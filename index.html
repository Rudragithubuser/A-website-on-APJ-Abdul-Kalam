Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam also called A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India. He was a great scientist and played a vital role in many ambitious projects, earning him the title of “Missile Man of India”. He was a man of simple living and higher thinking. He was keen on encouraging students to take actively participation in science and technology. He was a great writer and poet. He had authored many books mostly in the field of science, technology, and inspiration. His Ideologies and thoughts are the guiding force for the younger generations
